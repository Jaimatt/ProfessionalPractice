In an effort to connect with my peers in computer science, I’ve been attending some of the events hosted by the CS Club. Amongst these was their quiz night in April. The theme for this quiz was “2000s”, with questions focussing on this theme, as well as computer science and other topics. The team I was in did not win in the slightest. Perhaps we just weren’t entrenched enough into the dregs of 2000s internet memes to understand many of the references! 
Despite our embarrassing inadequacy at the quiz itself, the evening gave me the opportunity to get to know some other computer science students who were present and involved in the CS Club. Amongst these, I recall our team chatting with the Vice President of the club for some time about the quiz & CS Club in general.
Through attending events like these I feel I’m gradually building a professional network of students that will shortly be entering the workforce in my field.
