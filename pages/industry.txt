An industry night is not the sort of event I would ordinarily think to go to. So, for this activity, I must thank Professional Practice’s incentive. This particular industry night was organised by the CS Club, Mathematics Society, and various other STEM-focussed AUSA clubs. I found it to be an enjoyable evening (no, not just because it was at the wine centre), and I got the opportunity to network with my peers & have interesting discussions with the varied employers that were present on the night.
Initially – as my friends will attest – I was nervous about approaching companies, but I gradually garnered the confidence to have a chat with their representatives, and learned a lot about my opportunities post-graduation. Over the course of the evening, I spoke to a majority of the employers present. Amongst my highlights were Jane Street, who offer an awesome internship program, and SAAB. The representative of the latter spoke with me at length about his own role, SAAB’s graduate positions, and I particularly found value in our discussions around the defence industry as a whole.
It was an enlightening evening, providing me with an abundance of information about the wide world that exists beyond graduation. And, as a bonus, it gave me some practice for the far busier career expo the next day!
