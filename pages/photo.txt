As part of improving my professional online presence, I attended a Careers Hub LinkedIn photo-shoot. For this event, they offered students the ability to sign up online and then receive a professional photograph of them (taken at the Uni Hub) which can be used for LinkedIn profiles, resumes, job applications, and the like.
At the session I joined the queue, followed the guidance provided by the on-site photographers, and later was emailed the final image. The process itself was fairly straightforward, but the result is significantly more polished than a casual phone photo! Certainly, this is better suited to professional contexts.
There’s not too much else to say about this activity. Although it was brief, it should be stressed the importance of having these sorts of photos to create a professional digital presence. It gives the impression I care about how I’m perceived by potential employers, and is useful for both job applications & networking. I will certainly be using this photograph as part of my professional profiles moving forward (it’s already on my LinkedIn right now!).
